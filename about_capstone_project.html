About Capstone Project

The Capstone Project in your Communication Skills course at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology is an extensive research initiative designed to integrate and apply the knowledge and skills you have acquired throughout your studies. It involves addressing a real-world problem or research question, showcasing your ability to conduct independent research, analyze data, and present your findings effectively. Here is an elaboration on the elements and significance of the project based on the provided document:

Structure and Requirements:
• Resume: An updated resume in Kiswahili, English, and one indigenous African language. This showcases your professional readiness and multilingual capabilities.
• Autobiographical Essay: A comprehensive essay (20-30 pages) detailing your personal journey. This exercise hones your reflective writing skills and ability to articulate personal experiences and growth.
• Presentation on Autobiographical Essay: Creating a PowerPoint, Prezi, or Keystone presentation of your essay, which enhances your presentation skills and ability to summarize extensive content concisely.
• Video Presentation on Invention or Innovation: A 20-minute video with an abstract in Kiswahili, English, and one indigenous African language. This task demonstrates your ability to communicate technical content effectively and cater to a diverse audience.
• Presentation on Invention or Innovation: Similar to the video presentation, but focused on delivering content through a visual presentation format.
• Video Presentation on African Culture: A 20-minute video discussing an aspect of African culture and its relevance to your field of study, including gender awareness. This promotes cultural understanding and contextual application of knowledge.
• Presentation on African Culture: A PowerPoint, Prezi, or Keystone presentation reinforcing the importance of cultural awareness in professional practice.
• Video Presentation on Societal Challenges: A 20-minute video addressing modern societal challenges and proposing solutions. This task emphasizes problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
• Photographic Essay: An essay on a significant issue within the African community, using photography to support your narrative. This blends visual storytelling with analytical writing.
• Online Professional Presence: Evidence of professional activity on at least five online platforms (blog, vlog, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, etc.). This underscores the importance of maintaining a professional digital presence.
